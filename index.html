Who Is Elon Musk?

Elon Musk (born June 28, 1971, Pretoria, South Africa) South African-born
American entrepreneur who cofounded the electronic-payment firm PayPal and
formed SpaceX maker of launch vehicles and spacecraft. He was also one of the
first significant investors in, as well as chief executive officer of, the electric car
manufacturer Tesla. In addition, Musk acquired Twitter (later X) in 2022.

Early Life Of Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk was born to a South African father and a Canadian mother. He displayed an early talent for computers and
entrepreneurship. At age 12 he created a video game and sold it to a computer magazine. In 1988, after obtaining a
Canadian passport, Musk left South Africa because he was unwilling to support apartheid through compulsory military
service and because he sought the greater economic opportunities available in the United States.

Main Accomplishments

  • 1995 - Elon Musk along with his brother founded Zip2, an online business
    directory, used as an online alternative to the standard paper yellow pages.
  • 1999 - Elon Musk along with three other co-founders launched X.com,
    an online bank.
  • 2000 - Elon Musk's X.com merged with a software company called Confinity,
    creating PayPal
  • 2002 - Elon Musk founded the space exploration company SpaceX
    which focuses on commercial space travel and the colonization of Mars.
  • 2002 - Elon Musk founded the Musk Foundation which focuses on supporting renewable energy,
    pediatric research, and education.
  • 2004 - Elon Musk started leading one of his most popular roles in the brand Tesla,
    which he started as an investor but would later become CEO of the company in 2008.
  • 2006 - Some of Elon Musk's cousins started a solar energy company named SolarCity with
    Elon as their primary financial backer. This company would grow to become the leading
    residential solar installer in the U.S. by the mid-2010s.
  • 2015 - Elon Musk co-founded a non-profit organization called OpenAI with a for-profit
    artificial intelligence reaserch lab component. This company was made on the grounds
    to research AI and in Elon Musk's words: "develop friendly AI that supports humanity."
  • 2016 - Elon Musk co-founded an organization named Nueralink with the goal
    of working on "brain-machine interfaces."
  • 2016 - Elon Musk started a side-business as a subsidiary of SpaceX with the
    goal of dispelling city traffic via underground tunnels called the Boring
    Company.
  • 2022 - Elon Musk bought enough Twitter stock to be a majority shareholder
    for the company and made a move to buy the company by the end of April.
    While the deal was decided there is still speculation whether he will actually go
    through with it or not due to Musk wanting to back out on the grounds of
    Twitter having "too many bots." As of right now the deal is on hold but since
    the Elon Musk and Twitter made the deal, he might be made to purchase it
    through court.
